The Founder

Meet Jo

"True luxury is not found in what you see, but in what you feel. It is the aura that remains long after the last guest departs."

With over a decade of shaping Nairobi's most prestigious gatherings, Jo has established a reputation for meticulous detail and an uncompromising eye for elegance. Her approach blends architectural precision with organic grace.

The Aura Philosophy

At Eventful Aura, we believe that an event is a living entity. It breathes through the choice of linen, speaks through the direction of lighting, and thrives through the energy of its guests. My journey began with a simple observation: Nairobi’s event scene was filled with noise, but lacked silence—the kind of sophisticated quiet that allows beauty to speak for itself.

We don’t follow trends; we curate legacies. Every piece in our catalogue and every service we offer is filtered through our three pillars of excellence. We look for the 'Aura'—that intangible quality that transforms a venue into a memory.

"Curating moments that resonate with the soul of the host."

Our team is composed of visionaries, craftsmen, and logisticians who share this obsession. We are builders of atmospheres. Whether it is a private residence transformation or a grand gala at a historic estate, our signature remains the same: Effortless, Refined, and Uniquely Yours.
